exploreCSR 2023 summer: Computing Research

In person Workshop Date: May, 2023.

Welcome

The University of Nevada, Las Vegas Computer Science is proud to partner with Google Research to offer exploreCSR for a rich research experience for undergraduate students. We encourage students from historically-underrepresented groups to apply, including but not limited to -- Hispanic, women, students of color, first-generation college students, students from low socioeconomic backgrounds, and LGBTQ+ students.

Our goal is to expose students to all aspects of computing research from faculty from different universities, gain their confidence to pursue graduate studies, develop practical skills, and achieve a sense of belonging in computing research.

This workshop covers four research areas:

  1. data mining and artificial intelligence
  2. pervasive computing and healthcare
  3. distributed databases and its applications
  4. tensor decompositions and analysis

About

Who can apply
undergraduate students (including graduating seniors). The program in this year is in the US Pacific time zone.
Experience needed
no prior research experience necessary. Coursework in introduction of computer science, calculus, linear algebra, and probability are helpful.
Research activities
workshop participants will be paired with a faculty mentor throughout the two-day event
Time commitment
the workshop is two day events and 10 faculty will present and engage with students.
Stipend
meals will be provided during the workshop and travel fees would be reimbursed for participants.
